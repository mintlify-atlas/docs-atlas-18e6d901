---
title: Creating a New Account
description: Learn how to create a new wallet account in SubWallet Extension using a seed phrase
---

This guide will walk you through creating a new account in SubWallet Extension. When you create a new account, SubWallet generates a 12 or 24-word seed phrase that serves as the master key to your wallet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the account creation screen">
    Click the **Create Account** button or navigate to **Accounts** > **New Seed Phrase** from the extension menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the security warning">
    Read the security warning carefully. Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your account, so it's critical to store it safely.

    <Warning>
      Never share your seed phrase with anyone. SubWallet will never ask for your seed phrase. Anyone with access to your seed phrase has complete control over your funds.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your seed phrase">
    SubWallet will display a 12 or 24-word seed phrase. You have two options to save it:

    - **Write it down**: Copy each word in order on paper and store it in a secure location
    - **Download it**: Click the download button to save it as a file (ensure you store the file securely)

    <Note>
      The words are displayed in a specific order. You must record them in the exact sequence shown, as the order matters for account recovery.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm you've saved your seed phrase">
    Once you've securely stored your seed phrase, click the **I've kept it somewhere safe** button to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your account">
    Enter a memorable name for your account in the Account Name modal. This name helps you identify the account within SubWallet but doesn't affect your on-chain identity.

    <Tip>
      Choose a descriptive name like "Main Wallet" or "Trading Account" to easily distinguish between multiple accounts.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for account creation">
    SubWallet will create your account and generate addresses for all supported blockchain networks. This process may take a few moments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start using your new account">
    Once creation is complete, your new account is ready to use. You can now receive tokens, connect to dApps, and manage your crypto assets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Types

SubWallet supports two types of accounts when creating from a seed phrase:

- **Unified Account**: A single seed phrase that works across all supported chains (recommended for most users)
- **Solo Account**: Chain-specific accounts for specialized use cases

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Store your seed phrase offline">
    Write your seed phrase on paper and store it in a secure location like a safe. Never store it digitally where it could be hacked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use multiple copies">
    Consider creating multiple copies of your seed phrase and storing them in different secure locations as backup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verify your backup">
    Before sending significant funds to your new account, test the recovery process with your seed phrase to ensure it works correctly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
